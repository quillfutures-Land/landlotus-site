Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who We Are

Land Lotus is a brand operated by Quill Adventures LLC ("we," "us," or "our"), a land acquisition company based in the United States. We purchase rural and vacant land nationwide. Our website is landlotus.com.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, phone number, and email address
  • Property location and ownership information
  • Communications you send us via form, SMS, phone, or email

We may also receive your contact information through public property records or our direct mail and outreach programs.

How We Use Your Information

  • Contact you about a potential purchase offer for your land
  • Send follow-up SMS messages and emails as part of our acquisition process
  • Schedule calls or coordinate closing if a transaction moves forward
  • Improve our services and communications

SMS / Text Messaging

By responding to our outreach or submitting your contact information, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Land Lotus / Quill Adventures LLC. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.

  • Reply STOP to opt out of SMS messages at any time.
  • Reply HELP for assistance.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with service providers (e.g., title companies, closing agents) only as necessary to complete a transaction you have agreed to.

Data Retention

We retain contact information as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. You may request deletion at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal information we hold. Contact us at info@landlotus.com or 702-840-5350.
