Common Questions

Frequently asked questions.

How long does the process take?
From first contact to closing, the process typically takes 2–4 weeks. We can move faster if you need a quick close, or slower if you need more time.
What types of land do you buy?
We buy all types of rural and vacant land — wooded lots, desert parcels, farmland, recreational acreage, and more. If you're not sure, just reach out and we'll let you know.
Do I need to do anything to prepare the land?
No. We buy land as-is. You don't need to clean it up, make improvements, or do anything to prepare it for sale.
Are there any fees or commissions?
None. The offer we make is the amount you receive. We cover our own closing costs. There are no agent fees, commissions, or deductions.
Is there any obligation when I request an offer?
Absolutely not. Requesting an offer is completely free and you are under no obligation to accept. We want you to feel good about the decision.
